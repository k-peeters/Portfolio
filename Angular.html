Short explenation

During the first semester of the third year, I worked together with 4 other students for the course Angular to develop an application in Angular, this application had the purpose to plan a multi-day trip in an intuitive user-friendly way.

The app aims to revolutionize the way people plan and share their trips. By providing an intuitive and user-friendly interface, users can easily create and customize their own multi-day trips, choosing from various types such as hiking, hotel, or blended itineraries. Each trip is initially private, visible only to the creator, but users can opt to make it public, allowing others to discover and gain inspiration from their travel ideas. Non-registered users are greeted with a visually appealing homepage that encourages them to register and create their own trip. Once registered, users can add new trips, edit or delete existing ones, manage activities such as autoroutes, flights, museums, hotels, and restaurants, and share their trips with friends and family via a public link or email. Additionally, users can search for other trips using keywords such as destination, activity, or type of trip, making it easy to find inspiration for their next adventure.

In this application I mainly created the 2 trip pages, both public and private trips, in this I added the data I got from the API. I also did the UI/UX for the whole application, we got compliments for this during our presentation, because it is not too complicated and rather simplistic. I also took care of the hosting, the hosting of the frontend was done through Vercel and the Backend was hosted through Azure.
